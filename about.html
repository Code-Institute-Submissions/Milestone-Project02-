Welcome to Bora Bora VIP!

We have a collection of resorts that make up Bora Bora VIP Immerse yourself in the island experience while enjoying a resort adjusted to perfection. All our resort feature both overwater and land villas including a spa on a private island, a Lagoonarium, a fitness center, five restaurants and a swim-up bar. For the ultimate private getaway, reserve a beach villa. Bicycles allow guests the freedom to navigate the 17 hectares Resort and shuttle service to the main island makes it easy to explore Bora Bora.

At Bora Bora VIP!®, you can step off the deck of your luxury over-water villa into the warm embrace of a tranquil lagoon. Enjoy everything from scuba diving to sunset cruises on the surrounding reefs, while a pristine beach and an award-winning spa invite you to relax. From weddings in the glass-bottomed chapel to candlelit dinners beneath swaying palm trees, it’s the perfect setting in which to experience the romance of the South Pacific.
